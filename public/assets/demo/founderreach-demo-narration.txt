FounderReach turns one startup description into a live partnership pipeline. In the dashboard, every stage stays visible while the active agent moves into focus. First, TinyFish-powered agents scan messy websites to match relevant labs, grants, and investors. Next, the platform qualifies each lead by strategic fit, funding recency, and collaboration potential. Then FounderReach drafts personalized outreach with approvals still in the loop, so the founder stays in control. When replies come in, the system hands the run into scheduling and calendar operations. The result is a stateful workflow that helps founders move from web research to real conversations, faster.
